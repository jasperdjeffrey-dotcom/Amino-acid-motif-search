📋 Motif Pattern Syntax (Case-Insensitive)

  • C - Specific amino acid (C)
  • x - Any amino acid
  • [DN] - Either D or N
  • {FWY} - Any amino acid except F, W, or Y
  • A(2,3) - A appears 2 to 3 times consecutively
  • x(5) - Any amino acid repeated 5 times
  • - - Separator between pattern elements
